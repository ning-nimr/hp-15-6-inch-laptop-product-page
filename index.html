HP 15.6-inch Laptop with AMD Ryzen 3

Product Overview

Get ready for a totally intuitive experience with the HP 15.6-inch Laptop. This PC features a micro-edge HD touchscreen display that lets you control your device with a simple pinch, tap, or swipe on its flicker-free, 1366 x 768 resolution screen. Powered by an AMD Ryzen 3 7320U processor and AMD Radeon Graphics, it delivers accelerated performance for your work, creativity, and entertainment. Enjoy speedy, responsive performance with 8 GB of LPDDR5 RAM and store your files on the 128 GB PCIe NVMe M.2 SSD, which performs up to 15 times faster than a traditional hard drive. It comes with Windows 11 Home in S Mode for a secure computing experience, and includes one-touch access to Microsoft Copilot to boost your productivity. Your privacy is protected with a physical camera shutter and a dedicated microphone mute button.
